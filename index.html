Blueberry Snack Bars

Ingredients

Bottom Layer

  • 3/4 cup oat flour

  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp molasses

  • 1 cup pureed organic blueberries (frozen is fine but be sure to thaw first)

  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
